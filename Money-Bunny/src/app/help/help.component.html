Welcome to the home page help section!



You are currently on the home page. From here you can login or sign up. After that you will be redirected to the user menu from where you can manage your accounts and enjoy all that MoneyBunny has to offer.

Individual help pages are provided for all the menus to help you navigate the site.


Welcome to the login page help section!


You are currently on the login page, from here you can login into your account.

To do that you need to type the email you used to create your account and the account password.

In the end you need to click on the "Login" button.

If you do not have an account on our website yet, you can click the button "Sign up" and a form will appear.


Welcome to the user menu help page section!



If you click on "Select Registered Bank Account", a new page with its own help page will open, listing all of the Bank Accounts you added to our system.

If you click on "Update Profile", a new page with its own help page will open, listing your profile details such as name, email or password. You can update you personal information here.

If you click on "Open New Bank Account", a new page with its own help page will open, providing a form for opening a new Bank Account with one of our Partner Banks.


Welcome to the select bank account help page section!



On this page you can either click on a Bank Account already inserted in the app, make a request to open a new bank account at a MoneyBunny Partner Bank, or add an existing bank account to MoneyBunny. Each option will provide you with a new help page.

After you click on the existing Bank Account, you have to choose between Select This Account, Close This Account or Block This Account.



"Select This Account" redirects you to a different page where you can perform operations with this bank account.

"Rename This Account" allows you to enter a new nickname for the bank account.

"Block This Account" will block the account and forbid you from selecting it. You can unblock it whenever you want. Getting in touch with the bank about the issue that made you need to block the account is recommended.

"Close This Account" will send a request to close the account to the bank. A bank operator will have to review it and approve it, but until then you will be unable to acces the account or modify anything abput it. When the request is accepted, the account will disappear from the list.


Welcome to the select bank help page section!



On this page you can open a new Banking Account with Money-Bunny at one of our Partner Banks. Your request will be reviewed by a bank operator and, when accepted, your new bank account will appear among your registered accounts.

Insert a nickname for your new account in the Account Name field. This will be the name that appears in the list of your registered accounts. You can change it anytime.


Welcome to the add account help page section!



On this page you can add an existing Banking Account from one of our Partner Banks to Money-Bunny. Your request will be reviewed by a bank operator and, when accepted, your bank account will appear among your registered accounts.

You must provide the IBAN and the PIN of your account, as well as its type and currecy su that the Partner Bank can check its existence and validate that it is yours.

Insert a nickname for your new account in the Account Name field. This will be the name that appears in the list of your registered accounts. You can change it anytime.


Welcome to the user profile help page section!



On this page you can modify all your personal information. The password can be changed by introducing the new password in the two fields beneath the username, the ones filled with dot characters. The password must be introduced in both to make sure that you did not misstype it.

If the new values do not respect the required format or if you insert a username that belongs to another user you will be notified.

Once you are done click Update and you will receive a success notification and be returned to the user menu.


Welcome to the register help page section!



On this page you can register an account for our website and use it to manage multiple bank accounts. Make sure to complete each form accordingly and your account will be activated in no time.

We request some personal information to make interacting with the bank easier for you. When you request to open or close a bank account you will only be asked to provide information about the account and all the presonal data required by the bank wil be provided by us based on the information in you user profile.


Welcome to the account dashboard help page section!



On this page you can view your account dashboard. It offers a digest of your account details and cards for the operations you can do with this account. Selecting a card will redirect you to a menu dedicated to that operation.


Welcome to the transaction help page section!



On this page you can perform a transaction.

You must provide the Destination IBAN as well as the amount you wish to transfer. The amount of money currently in your account is displayed on the page and you will be warned if you insert a larger sum.

The IBANs of the accounts you have already interacted with will be saved and will appear in the Frequent Accounts drop-down menu.

You can also schedule recurring payments by introducing the number of days between each payment in the Recurring Payment field. If you are paying an utility bill, you can specify the Invoice Number.

Once you are done, click on "Complete Transaction".


Welcome to the transaction report help page section!



On this page you can view your transactions and also download them as a .PDF file.

You can choose if you wish Today, Last Week or Last Month's transactions. They will be displayed as a table specifying the Source, Destination, Amount and Date. To download as a .PDF file click on "Download as PDF".

You can see if a payment is recurrent and how often it repeats and you can cancell the recurrency by clicking the red X next to it. If you do so the payment will not be repeated anymore, but it will still appear in your report.


Welcome to the currency conversion help page section!



On this page you can convert from any currency into the currency of your account.

Insert the sum you want to obtain, slect the desired currency from the Drop-Down Menu and we will calculate how much money you would have to substract from your account.

The Converted Ammount field will show you how much your desired sum means in the currency of your account, as well as how much money you have in your account and the currecny of your account. Like so it is very easy to see if you could afford to pay the desired sum.

If you have enough money, you can click "Insert this amount into a new transaction" to use the conversion and send money to an account in a different currency.
