Welcome to the home page help section!



You are currently on the home page, from here you can login, view your account balance, schedule online payments, secure online transations, open new accounts or the last option: block cards or close accounts.

An individual help page for each of these will appear as soon as you click on your preferred option.

Welcome to the login page help section!


You are currently on the login page, from here you can login into your account.


To do that you need to type your username in the box that is labeled "Username" and your password in the box that is labeled "Password".

In the end you need to click on the "Login" button.

If you want your credentials remembered for the next time you acces our website, you can opt to tick the option "Remember me", if not, just leave it unselected.

If you do not remember your password, click "Forgot password?" and a special form with clear instructions will appear on your screen.

If you do not have an account on our website yet, you can click the button "Sign up" and a form will appear.

Welcome to the logged in help page section!



If you click on "Select Registered Bank Account", a new page with its own help page will open, listing all of your registered bank accounts.

If you click on "Update Profile", a new page with its own help page will open, listing your profile details such as name, email or password.

If you click on "Open New Bank Account", a new page with its own help page will open, listing a group of banks and forms to choose and complete.

Welcome to the select bank account help page section!



On this page you either have to click on an existing Bank Account, or click the green button below to create a new Banking account. Each option will provide you with a new help page.

Welcome to the select bank help page section!



On this page you can choose from which bank to open a new Banking Account with Money-Bunny. After you click the desired bank, you will be prompted with a series of self-explainatory forms to complete.

Welcome to the user profile help page section!



On this page you can modify your personal information, including Full Name, Email Address, Password, CNP and more. Make sure to complete the forms accordingly.

Welcome to the register help page section!



On this page you can register an account for our website for use with multiple bank accounts. Make sure to complete each form accordingly and your account will be activated in no time.

Welcome to the account dashboard help page section!



On this page you can view your account dashboard. This includes your current account bank, IBAN, Account Type, your Balance and many other actions such as transactions of paying bills.

Welcome to the transaction help page section!



On this page you can perform a transaction. You must provide the Destination IBAN as well as the amount you wish to transfer. The last step is to click on "Complete Transaction".
